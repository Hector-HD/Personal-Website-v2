ParkingFinder App

Summary

ParkingFinder App March 2017 - March 2017
EmergenTech Hack-A-Thon Tempe, AZ

    During the Spring of 2017 I attended Arizona State University's Hack-A-Thon competition held by EmergenTech. My group and I decided we wanted to help people, reduce the carbon foot print and impact our community by solving a common issue. Then it dawned on me on how terrible finding a parking spot at ASU was. So we decided to make finding a parking spot a simpler task. Our idea would help people waste less gas, reduce city traffic, and organize parking spaces for events. We then had to figure out what technology we needed in order to make this possible.
